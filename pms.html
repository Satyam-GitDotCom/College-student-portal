    🗺️ Step-by-Step Application Process

    1

    Register on PMS Portal

    Visit pmsonline.bih.nic.in → Click "New Student Registration" → Fill basic details and get login credentials.

    2

    Fill Application Form Online

    Log in → Fill personal details, education details, bank account details (must be in your name), and upload documents.

    3

    Upload Required Documents

    Upload scanned copies of: Caste certificate, Income certificate, Aadhaar card, Bank passbook, College ID, fee receipt, and photos.

    4

    Submit & Print Application

    Review all details carefully → Submit → Print the filled application form. Keep it safe.

    5

    College Verification

    Submit printed form + original documents to your college's scholarship section. The Nodal Officer will verify and forward.

    6

    Track Status & Receive Amount

    Track status on the portal using your application ID. Amount is transferred directly to your bank account via DBT.
